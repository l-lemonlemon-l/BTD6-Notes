Bloon Intel

DDT MOAB-class

Dark Dirigible Titan. First appears round 90 (88 in Alt Bloons Rounds).

400 HP (800 fortified) — scales to ~480 by round 90. Moves slightly faster than Ceramics.

Has Black + Lead + Camo all at once. Pops into 4 Camo Regrow Ceramics. Immune to attacks that only reach BFBs.

Needs: camo detection, lead-popping, black-popping — all three or it walks through.

Camo Property

Not a bloon type — a property. Any bloon up to Ceramic can have it, plus DDTs, tier 2/4+ Golden Bloons, and Phayze by default.

Invisible to towers without camo detection — they ignore it entirely, and stray projectiles phase through with no damage.

First appears round 24 (round 5 in Alt Bloons Rounds).

Fixes: permanent detection (Radar Scanner, Night Vision Goggles, Etienne, heroes like Ezili/Sauda/Psi/Silas) or decamo pulses (Signal Flare, Submerge and Support, Super Brittle, Camo Trap) — decamos have downtime, so dense rounds can slip through the gaps.

BAD MOAB-class

Big Airship of Doom. First appears round 100 (99 in Alt Bloons Rounds). Strongest non-boss bloon.

Same size/speed as a ZOMG. Immune to blowback, knockback, slow, and stun (few exceptions like Time Stop).

55,760 RBE (98,360 fortified) base — scales to 67,200 by round 100.

Releases 2 ZOMGs + 3 DDTs on pop. Round 100 usually solved with burst damage (Spike Storm) rather than sustained DPS.

Towers

Primary

Dart Monkey — throws darts (no Lead/frozen). Paths: spiked balls with high pierce / more darts + temp Super Monkey conversion / crossbow bolts.

Boomerang Monkey — curved boomerangs, up to 4 pops. Paths: bouncing glaives / faster attacks + burst ability / straight-line stalling glaives.

Bomb Shooter — AoE bombs, pops Lead + frozen, not Black. Paths: bigger stunning explosions / MOAB-shredding missiles / cluster bombs.

Tack Shooter — 8-directional burst, short range.

Ice Monkey — AoE freeze + damage, no MOAB-class by default.

Glue Gunner — slows single targets with long-lasting glue, no direct damage by default.

Desperado — pistol default. Paths: revolver (more rounds/shot) / rifle (long range) / shotgun (close range).

Military

Sniper Monkey — global range, single target, no Camo by default.

Monkey Sub — homing darts underwater. Paths: submerge for support utility / missile variants / dart buffs.

Monkey Buccaneer — dual cannons, water only. Paths: faster fire + attack planes / cannons that hook in MOAB-class / cash generation.

Monkey Ace — flies a fixed path, drops darts.

Heli Pilot — follows cursor, flexible positioning.

Mortar Monkey — long-range AoE, needs target designation.

Dartling Gunner — follows cursor, rapid fire, gift-box unlock.

Magic

Wizard Monkey — magic bolts, no Purple/Lead by default. Paths: stronger bolts / fire attacks / dark magic (camo reveal + undead summons).

Super Monkey — dart volley, unlocked lvl 15-20. Paths: Sun God powers / robotic transformation / dual dark blades + teleport.

Ninja Monkey — shuriken, innate Camo detection.

Alchemist — acid flasks, pops everything but no Camo detection. Paths: buff potions for other towers / stronger acid + explosions / turn bloons to gold.

Druid — thorn volley, no frozen/Lead by default. Paths: storm (lightning/tornadoes) / jungle (vines + cash) / wrath (speed/damage).

Mermonkey — trident splash, land or water (better in water). Paths: stronger tridents + freeze/seek / Abyssal Creature tentacles / entrancing song that buffs towers.

Support

Banana Farm — generates cash, no attack.

Spike Factory — passive track spikes, can hit Camo without detection.

Monkey Village — buffs range/pierce/discounts for nearby towers.

Engineer Monkey — sentry gun + deployable traps.

Beast Handler — melee by default, upgrades add up to 2 of 3 beasts (land/water/air) to attack instead.
