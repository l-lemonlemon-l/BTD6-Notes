Bloon Intel

DDT MOAB-class

Dark Dirigible Titan. First appears round 90 (88 in Alt Bloons Rounds).

400 HP (800 fortified) — scales to ~480 by round 90. Moves slightly faster than Ceramics.

Has Black + Lead + Camo all at once. Pops into 4 Camo Regrow Ceramics. Immune to attacks that only reach BFBs.

Needs: camo detection, lead-popping, black-popping — all three or it walks through.

Camo Property

Not a bloon type — a property. Any bloon up to Ceramic can have it, plus DDTs, tier 2/4+ Golden Bloons, and Phayze by default.

Invisible to towers without camo detection — they ignore it entirely, and stray projectiles phase through with no damage.

First appears round 24 (round 5 in Alt Bloons Rounds).

Fixes: permanent detection (Radar Scanner, Night Vision Goggles, Etienne, heroes like Ezili/Sauda/Psi/Silas) or decamo pulses (Signal Flare, Submerge and Support, Super Brittle, Camo Trap) — decamos have downtime, so dense rounds can slip through the gaps.

BAD MOAB-class

Big Airship of Doom. First appears round 100 (99 in Alt Bloons Rounds). Strongest non-boss bloon.

Same size/speed as a ZOMG. Immune to blowback, knockback, slow, and stun (few exceptions like Time Stop).

55,760 RBE (98,360 fortified) base — scales to 67,200 by round 100.

Releases 2 ZOMGs + 3 DDTs on pop. Round 100 usually solved with burst damage (Spike Storm) rather than sustained DPS.
